**Date:** February 3, 2026  
**Status:** Implemented in Wave 4  
**Reference:** See `UI-DESIGN-SYSTEM.md` for complete design system documentation

### Design Philosophy

Shifted from bordered cards to a modern, data-focused aesthetic inspired by Notion and Ramp:
- **Shadows over borders** - Cards use `shadow-sm` with `hover:shadow-md` instead of visible borders
- **Sentence case titles** - All section headers use sentence case (not UPPERCASE)
- **Generous spacing** - Increased padding and breathing room throughout
- **Soft typography** - Font weights and sizes create hierarchy without heavy styling
- **Clean backgrounds** - Gray-50 page background with white cards for contrast

### Key Changes from V2

**1. Card Styling**
- Removed: `border border-gray-200`
- Added: `shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow rounded-xl`
- Padding increased from `p-3-4` to `p-5-8` depending on content

**2. Typography**
- Section titles: `text-xl font-bold UPPERCASE` â†’ `text-lg font-semibold Sentence case`
- Card titles: Normalized casing (e.g., "ENTERPRISE" â†’ "Enterprise")
- Removed colons from KPI labels

**3. Spacing**

- Section margins: `mb-4` â†’ `mb-6` for better separation
